Why Students Love CleanCrush

Designed specifically for academic workflows with student privacy as the top priority

Privacy First

Unlike other cleaners, CleanCrush never reads your file contents. We respect your academic work.

  • Metadata-only scanning
  • Local processing only
  • Protected folder system

Exam-Aware

Understands academic cycles and helps you stay organized during and after exams.

  • Auto-detects exam periods
  • Tracks study files automatically
  • Smart post-exam cleanup

Gamified Cleanup

Making digital organization fun with achievements and progress tracking.

  • Streaks & achievements
  • Cleanliness score (0-100)
  • Encouraging messages
